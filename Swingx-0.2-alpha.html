instructions
  • Go left with "A" or "left-arrow"
  • Go right with "D" or "right-arrow"
  • Aim up with "W" or "up-arrow"
  • Aim down with "S" or "down-arrow"
  • Jump with "Space"
  • Grip and Swing with "Left Control" or "Return/enter"
  • zoom in and out by pressing "Left shift"
  • change the length of the rope by Scrolling
  • To complete a level you need to find all the red circles!
